A warm welcome to Hotel Manofa, Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the city of youth, great architecture and, of course, exciting night-life and great business opportunities. It does not matter if you come to Amsterdam to party, or to explore all its beautiful sights like the world-famous canals or historical buildings. Amsterdam and the people who live there extend you a friendly welcome. Most people in Amsterdam speak English as a second language and are always able and willing to help you to find your way in their city.

Hotel Manofa, situated in the heart of Amsterdam, is a two-star hotel. We offer comfortable accommodation to every kind of traveller to Amsterdam, be they here for business or pleasure. All our rooms have a private bathroom. Our hotel offers several types of rooms for one to four guests. We have no elevator, but the well-known Amsterdam stairs.

We have a 24-hour multilingual reception staff that will warmly welcome you to our hotel and help you with your visiting plans.
